ABOUT US

Welcome to Aquapaw, where pet care innovation meets the heart of the pet-owner bond, paw to paw. We understand the importance of pretty grooming and pet well-being. Whether you're a seasoned canine groomer or a pet parent, Aquapaw has you covered with professional-quality tools making every session delightful.

Aquapaw's story is one of innovation, passion, and dedication to our furry friends. It all began with the simple idea that pet bathing could be made easier by rethinking the process from the animal's perspective. In 2017, we brought this idea to life with the launch of our first product, the Aquapaw Pet Bathing Tool.

Originally founded by Daniel Lentz, Aquapaw is now fully owned and operated by Cap Hill Brands. Daniel's journey began during his college years, where he grew up bathing his family's dogs. His passion for solving everyday problems led him to develop the Aquapaw, fueled by his expertise as a professional Industrial Designer.

Leading our business development is Francois de Ginestet, whose strategic vision has been instrumental in expanding Aquapaw's reach into international markets. Francois brings a wealth of experience and linguistic prowess, making him a valuable asset to our team.

And let's not forget Cali, our Chief Morale Officer. While she may seem like supporting cast, Cali truly runs the show. She provides invaluable feedback on our future product ideas during her weekly baths and finds it hard to focus when tennis balls are close by.

Daniel Lentz

We hope you will join us at Aquapaw, where every splash is a step towards a happier, healthier bond with your pet.
