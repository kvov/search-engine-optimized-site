HOW TO INSTALL

Aquapaw Pet Bathing Tool Set-Up

Installing in the Shower

  1. Unscrew the shower head from the shower pipe.
  2. Wrap the shower pipe with the provided thread sealing tape.
  3. Attach the shower adapter onto the shower pipe until it is securely fitted, avoiding over-tightening.
  4. Reattach your shower head onto the threads of the shower adapter.
  5. Utilize the push-button switch on the adapter to alternate between your regular shower and the Aquapaw.

Installing on the Garden Hose

  1. Screw the garden hose adapter onto your garden hose or spigot.
  2. Press the quick-connect at the end of the Aquapaw hose onto the adapter until it clicks into place.
  3. You're ready to go!
  4. Note: Setup instructions may differ for our Pro Grooming tool. For inquiries, please contact us at mail@aquapaw.com.
